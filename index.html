How to Play

🕹️ Movement & Aiming

Your character follows your mouse cursor. Move your mouse to walk — the further your cursor is from your character, the faster you move. Keep your cursor on top of your character to stop.

Your character always faces your cursor. Abilities fire in the direction you're aiming, so point at your opponent before casting.

⚔️ Abilities

You have 5 ability slots shown at the bottom of the screen. Press 1–5 to use them. Each ability has a 3-second cooldown after use (Reflection is 15s). You can customise your loadout from the main menu by clicking any slot.

White Ball — 10 damage, fast projectile

A quick, straight-moving projectile. Great for consistent chip damage and keeping pressure on your opponent.

Fireball — 20 damage, slower projectile

Deals double the damage of White Ball but travels slower. Best used at close range or when your opponent is slowed.

Knockback — 0 damage, pushes opponent away

Fires a ring that launches your opponent away from it. Use it to push enemies into walls or off-position. No damage, but great for disruption.

Impulse — 0 damage, pulls opponent toward you

The opposite of Knockback — pulls your opponent toward the ring. Combo it with a Fireball right after to deal big damage.

Snowball — 10 damage, slows on hit

Deals damage and slows your opponent for 1.5 seconds, cutting their movement speed in half. Follow up immediately with Fireball for easy hits.

Landmine — 25 damage, proximity trap

Throws a mine that slides forward and stops. It arms only when an enemy steps inside the ring, then explodes after 0.5 seconds. The owner cannot trigger it. Great for area denial and baiting opponents.

Dash — instant teleport forward

Instantly moves your character forward in the direction you're facing. Use it to dodge incoming projectiles or close the gap on your opponent.

Heal Zone — restores 25 HP over 5 seconds

Places a green healing circle at your feet. Stand inside it to regenerate health. You can move while healing, but leaving the circle stops the effect.

Reflection — blocks and reflects projectiles for 3 seconds

Activates a shield that absorbs or reflects incoming projectiles back at your opponent. Has a 15-second cooldown — use it wisely when you expect a big attack.

Target — 50 damage, homing projectile

Fires a slow-moving projectile that tracks your opponent. Deals massive damage if it connects. Hard to dodge at close range.

❤️ Health & Regeneration

You start with 200 HP. If you haven't taken damage for 10 seconds, you slowly regenerate health automatically. Use Heal Zone to speed up recovery mid-fight.

📊 ELO & Ranking

Every player starts at 1000 ELO. You gain ELO for winning and lose ELO for losing. The amount gained or lost depends on the difference between your ELO and your opponent's — beating a higher-ranked player earns more. Sign in to save your ELO permanently. Guests can still earn ELO but it resets when you leave.

🤖 Training Mode

While waiting for an opponent, a "Train" button appears at the bottom of the screen. Click it to fight a bot while you wait. The bot's difficulty scales with your ELO — the higher your rating, the harder it plays. When a real opponent is found, you're automatically moved into the real match.

💡 Tips

• Combo Snowball into Fireball — slow them first, then land the big hit.

• Use Dash to dodge, not just to chase. Reacting to a Fireball with a Dash can save your life.

• Landmines are best placed in doorways or narrow corridors where your opponent has to walk through.

• Impulse + Fireball is a deadly combo — pull them in, then blast them.

• Reflection is most powerful when your opponent has just fired a Fireball or Target at you.

• Keep moving. A stationary player is easy to hit.
