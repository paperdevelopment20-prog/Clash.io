How to Play

🕹️ Section 1: How to Move and Aim

In this game, your character is the circle in the middle of the screen. You do not need to press any buttons to walk; your character simply follows your mouse cursor.

To Move: Move your mouse toward where you want to go, and your character will walk in that direction.

To Stop: Keep your mouse cursor directly on top of your character.

To Aim: Your character has two smaller circles (hands). These hands always point toward your mouse cursor. To aim a spell at an opponent, simply point your mouse at them.

⚔️ Section 2: Your Five Abilities

You have five different powers shown at the bottom of your screen. To use them, press the number keys (1, 2, 3, 4, or 5) on your keyboard. After using a power, its icon will turn dark for a few seconds while it "recharges" before you can use it again.

The Attack Abilities

Key 1 - Basic Blast: This fires a small, fast white energy ball. Since it recharges the quickest, you can fire this constantly to keep your opponent busy.

Key 2 - Fireball: This is your strongest attack. It is a large, orange ball of fire that deals a lot of damage. It moves slower than the basic blast, so try to use it when your opponent is close to you.

The Tactical & Survival Abilities

Key 3 - Snowball: This is a strategic tool. When it hits an opponent, it coats them in ice and slows them down. This makes them a much easier target because they cannot run away from your other attacks.

Key 4 - Dash: This is your "quick move" button. It makes your character leap forward instantly. Use this to jump out of the way if someone fires a fireball at you.

Key 5 - Heal Zone: This is how you fix your health. It places a green circle on the ground. As long as you stand inside the circle, your green health bar will fill back up.

🏆 Section 3: A Simple Strategy for Beginners

The secret to winning your first match is combining your powers instead of just pressing one button. Follow this three-step plan:

The Setup: Point your mouse at the enemy and press 3 (Snowball). This slows them down so they can't dodge.

The Strike: While they are moving slowly, immediately press 2 (Fireball) and 1 (Basic Blast).

The Recovery: If you get hit, press 4 (Dash) to jump away to a safe corner, then press 5 (Heal Zone) to get your health back.

Remember: Always keep your mouse moving! A character that stands still is very easy to hit. Good luck in the arena!
